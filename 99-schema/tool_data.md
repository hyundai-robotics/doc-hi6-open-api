## tool_data

### Description

Robot's tool data.

|key|description|
|:---:|:---|
|`x`|X position (mm)|
|`y`|Y position (mm)|
|`z`|Z position (mm)|
|`rx`|RX Angle (deg.)|
|`ry`|RY Angle (deg.)|
|`rz`|RZ Angle (deg.)|
|`mass`|weight (kg.)|
|`cx`|Center of gravity at X position (mm)|
|`cy`|Center of gravity at Y position (mm)|
|`cz`|Center of gravity at Z position (mm)|
|`ixx`| inertial X (kgm2)|
|`iyy`| inertial Y (kgm2)|
|`izz`| inertial Z (kgm2)|
|`mass_esti`|Load estimate weight (kg.)|
